Special Event

Celebrating 50 Years of Ministry

Saturday, August 8, 2026 · 4:00 PM

Save the date. On Saturday, August 8, 2026, we will gather to celebrate fifty years of faithful ministry, honoring the life and legacy of our late Bishop George L. Dowell and celebrating Senior Pastor Shanae Dowell.

It will be a day of worship, remembrance, gratitude, and joy as we thank God for five decades of shepherding, teaching, and love poured into this congregation and this city.

Tickets are on sale now, and seating is limited. Reserve your seat and be part of this special celebration.
