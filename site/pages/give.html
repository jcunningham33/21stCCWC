Give

“Every man according as he purposeth in his heart, so let him give; not grudgingly, or of necessity: for God loveth a cheerful giver.” — 2 Corinthians 9:7

Ways to Give

Your tithes and offerings make it possible for 21st CCWC to preach the gospel, disciple believers, and serve our community through outreach like our monthly food distribution. Every gift — large or small — matters.

In Person

You may give during any Sunday service at 421 N. Vermilion St., Danville, IL.

By Mail

Checks can be made payable to 21st Century Christian Worship Center and mailed to:
421 N. Vermilion St.
Danville, IL

Online

An online giving option is being prepared. Please contact the church office for the most current online giving instructions.

Tax Information

21st Century Christian Worship Center is a registered nonprofit religious organization. Federal EIN: 80‑0929434. Donations are tax‑deductible to the extent allowed by law.
