About Our Church

A body of radical believers in Danville, Illinois, rooted in the Word, led by the Spirit, and called to serve.

Our Story

21st Century Christian Worship Center was founded in 2014 in Danville, Illinois as a place where the uncompromised gospel of Jesus Christ would be preached and the power of the Holy Spirit experienced. From a small group of believers committed to prayer and the Word, the ministry has grown into a multi-generational family that gathers each week at 421 N. Vermilion Street, Danville, IL 61832.

Today, our congregation continues that same vision: presenting our bodies as a living sacrifice (Romans 12:1), making disciples, and bringing the love of Christ to our neighbors throughout Danville and Vermilion County.

What We Believe

  • We believe the Bible is the inspired and infallible Word of God.
  • We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • We believe in the deity, virgin birth, sinless life, atoning death, bodily resurrection, and personal return of Jesus Christ.
  • We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone.
  • We believe in the present-day work of the Holy Spirit, who empowers believers to live, love, and serve.
  • We believe the local church is the family of God, called to worship together, grow together, and reach the lost together.

Meet the Leadership

Bishop George L. Dowell

Bishop George L. Dowell is a passionate shepherd dedicated to guiding our congregation in faith, hope, and love. With over 50 years of pastoral experience, he brings a deep commitment to serving our community and nurturing spiritual growth. His passion is to teach, train, and empower leaders.

Pastor Shanae C. Dowell

Pastor Shanae C. Dowell's journey in ministry began with a calling to serve the needs of others over 50 years ago. Her compassionate leadership style and dynamic preaching inspires us to live out our faith boldly. Known for her engaging sermons rooted in scripture, Pastor Shanae has a gift for prophecy, healing, and a passion for Intercessory Prayer. Her teachings challenge us to grow in our relationship with God and each other.

Pastor Larry Greer

Pastor Larry Greer is actively involved in local outreach programs, seeking to make a tangible difference in the lives of those in need with over 35 years of experience. He believes in the power of faith in action. Pastor Greer serves as the Worship Service coordinator, Men's Leadership, community services, and maintenance.

Pastors Christopher and Cicely Knight

Pastors Christopher and Cicely Knight are a ministry duo with over 35 years of ministry and Christian experience. They are always available to listen, pray, and offer guidance. The Knights coordinate the media, outreach events, church administration, teachings/preaching, singing and spoken word. Feel free to reach out to them via email or social media platforms.

Join Us This Sunday

Experience each pastor's own unique way of presenting the Word as they preach in a rotation. We invite you to join us for worship this Sunday at 9:15 AM for Sunday Morning Teaching and 10:00 AM for Sunday Morning Worship Service, where you'll find a community ready to embrace you with open arms.

Our Mission

To love God with all our heart, mind, soul, and strength, and to love our neighbor as ourselves. We exist to bring people into a real relationship with Jesus Christ, to disciple them in the Word, and to send them out to serve their families, their workplaces, and their communities.
