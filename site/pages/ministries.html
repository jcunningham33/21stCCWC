Worship

Sunday Worship

Come as you are. Leave encouraged.

Sunday mornings at 21st Century begin with Back to Basics School at 9:15 AM, a time of practical teaching that grounds us in the foundations of our faith. At 10:00 AM we gather for Sunday Morning Worship, where heartfelt praise, prayer, and the preaching of the Word fill the house.

Our pastors preach in rotation, so each week you will hear the Word presented with a fresh voice and a personal touch. Wherever you are on your journey, there is a seat here for you.

Meeting Time

Sundays at 9:15 AM and 10:00 AM

What to Expect

  • Anointed praise and worship
  • Prayer for every need
  • Preaching in pastoral rotation
  • A warm, welcoming church family
Discipleship

Wednesday Bible Study

Go deeper in the Word, together.

Every Wednesday evening we open the Scriptures together and take our time with them. Bible Study is a place to ask questions, share what you are learning, and let the Word of God speak into everyday life.

Bring your Bible, bring your questions, and bring a friend. Every age and every stage of faith is welcome at the table.

Meeting Time

Wednesdays at 6:00 PM

What to Expect

  • Verse by verse teaching
  • Open discussion
  • Prayer together
  • Practical application for daily life
Prayer

Prayer & Intercession

We believe prayer changes things.

Prayer is the heartbeat of our church. Our intercessors stand in the gap for our congregation, our city, and every request entrusted to us. Pastor Shanae C. Dowell carries a deep passion for intercessory prayer, and that passion has shaped this ministry from the beginning.

Need prayer? Send us your request through the contact page or share it with us in person on Sunday. Nothing is too small, and nothing is too big.

How to Reach Us

Submit a request any time

What to Expect

  • Confidential intercession
  • Agreement in faith
  • Encouragement and follow up
  • Prayer during every service
Community Care

Community Food Distribution

Feeding our neighbors, no questions asked.

Each month our church gives away free food boxes to families in Danville and the surrounding area. It is one of the simplest and most practical ways we know to put faith into action and love our neighbors well.

Everyone is welcome. If you would like to receive a box, volunteer your hands, or donate toward the next giveaway, reach out and we will get you connected.

When

Monthly. Contact us for this month’s date.

What to Expect

  • Free food boxes
  • Friendly volunteers
  • No requirements and no paperwork
  • Prayer if you would like it

Young Adult Ministry

The Bridge

Where Faith Meets Real Life

The Bridge is the young adult ministry of 21st Century Christian Worship Center, created for those seeking a deeper relationship with God through authentic community, biblical discussion, and spiritual growth.

Our gatherings provide a welcoming environment where young adults can ask honest questions, explore God's Word, and encourage one another through every season of life. Each session is centered on relevant topics, open discussion, practical application, and building meaningful relationships with Christ and with one another.

Whether you're new to your faith or have been walking with Christ for years, The Bridge is a place to grow, belong, and be strengthened.

The Bridge is led by Deacon Jordan Cunningham and Sis. Ahmani Cunningham.

"Bridging the gap between where you are and where God is calling you to be."

Meeting Time

Thursdays at 6:00 PM

Led By

Deacon Jordan Cunningham
Sis. Ahmani Cunningham

What to Expect

  • Biblical discussion
  • Interactive Q&A
  • Real-life application
  • Community and fellowship
  • A welcoming environment for young adults
Marriage Ministry

Marriage Matters

Building Stronger Marriages. Centered on Christ.

Marriage Matters is a monthly ministry designed to encourage, equip, and strengthen marriages through biblical wisdom, practical teaching, and authentic conversations.

Each gathering features experienced speakers and couples who share real-life stories, biblical principles, and practical insights for building healthy, Christ-centered marriages. Every session concludes with an open Q&A, giving attendees the opportunity to engage in meaningful conversation and receive practical guidance for everyday life.

Whether you're newly married or have shared decades together, Marriage Matters offers a welcoming space to invest in your relationship and grow together in Christ.

"What therefore God hath joined together, let not man put asunder."
— Mark 10:9 (KJV)

Meeting Time

Fourth Saturday of every month at 6:00 PM

What to Expect

  • Inspiring guest speakers
  • Biblical teaching
  • Real-life marriage experiences
  • Live audience Q&A
  • Fellowship and community
