Our Ministries

From the worship service on Sunday morning to the streets of our city Monday through Saturday, here's where life and faith come together at 21st CCWC.

♪

Worship & Praise

Spirit-led worship every Sunday morning at 10:00 AM. Our worship team leads the congregation into the presence of God through song, prayer, and praise.

📖

Bible Study

Mid-week study of the Scriptures for spiritual growth, accountability, and discipleship. Contact us for the current weekly schedule.

🙏

Prayer Ministry

Corporate and personal prayer is the foundation of our church. Submit a prayer request and our intercessors will agree with you in faith.

🍞

Community Food Distribution

Each month our church gives away free food boxes to families in Danville and the surrounding area. Everyone is welcome — no questions asked.

♀

Women's Ministry

A sisterhood of women growing in Christ together — through Bible study, mentoring, fellowship, and service.

♂

Men's Ministry

Men of God called to be husbands, fathers, leaders, and disciples. Real fellowship around the Word.

★

Youth & Children

Equipping the next generation to know Jesus and walk with Him. Age-appropriate teaching, activities, and mentorship.

✚

Outreach & Missions

Beyond our walls — local outreach in Danville, partnerships with community organizations, and support for missions.
