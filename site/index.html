What to Expect

A real church for real people.

We are a Spirit-filled congregation that believes the Word of God transforms lives. Whether you've been in church your whole life or you're walking in for the first time, there is a seat for you here.

✝

Christ-Centered Worship

Anointed praise, prayer, and preaching of the uncompromised Word. Expect the presence of God to meet you.

♡

A Family of Believers

Real relationships across generations. We care for each other in season and out, in joy and in trial.

★

Serving Our City

Monthly food distributions and ongoing outreach in Danville and Vermilion County. We love our community.

Our Pastors

Bishop George L. Dowell & Co‑Pastor Shanae Dowell

Bishop George L. Dowell and Co‑Pastor Shanae Dowell shepherd the congregation of 21st Century Christian Worship Center, where they have served the people of Danville with more than five decades of combined ministry experience. Their preaching is rooted in Scripture, rich in the prophetic, and full of compassion for the lost and the hurting.
